Welcome to the Original Conference App

This application is being created for the 2013 Original Women's Conference. Original Conference is hosted by Original Women, a ministry of Rockford First from April 18-20, 2013. Rockford First is located in Rockford, Illinois in the state-line area about an hour northwest of Chicago. Rockford First is a church that emphasizes a pursuit after God, authentic relationships, and a life of purpose. It is made up of a vibrant and growing group of individuals who want to make a difference in their communities, see God do big things, and connect with others in a meaningful way. Rockford First is more than just a friendly church, it’s a family church.

Speakers

JEN DEWEERDT

Jen DeWeerdt serves with her husband, Jeremy, in the leadership of Rockford First. She is honored to play a key role in the very church she grew up in. Jen leads Original Women, the women’s ministry of Rockford First, which presents the Original Women’s Conference every April. Thousands of women have discovered their destiny and design through these powerful conferences.

Jen and Jeremy found a social sector organization called Earthbeat, Inc. Earthbeat is a non-profit organization which mobilizes youth and young adults to give back to their communities through volunteerism, especially related to environmental service projects. Today, one of her passions is to teach, encourage, and empower women to step into their God-given callings, regardless of their profession.

Jen and Jeremy have been married since 1995 and have three sons: Caden, Connor, and Paxton.

NANCY ALCORN

Nancy Alcorn is an author, international speaker, and founder and president of Mercy Ministries. Mercy Ministries exists to provide opportunities for young women who face life-controlling problems to experience God's unconditional love, forgiveness, and life-transforming power. Nancy's passion to help women find freedom has extended worldwide as Mercy Ministries now has locations across the United States, as well as Australia, Peru, and the United Kingdom.

SHEILA WALSH

Sheila Walsh is a powerful speaker, best-selling author (with more than 4 million books sold), and singer who has reached countless women by combining honesty, vulnerability, and humor with God's Word. Her transparency is breathtaking as she shares the real issues she, and many women, deal with every day.

She is currently completing her Master's degree in Divinity and lives in Texas with her husband, Barry, son, Christian, and her two little dogs, Belle and Tink.
